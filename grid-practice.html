Who is BidRocket?

We help non-profits grow by making fundraising easy and accessible

BidRocket's launch in 2019 is the evolution of over 20 years in the world of fundraising and software development. Formerly called Auction Source Austin, our rebranding reflects a new trajectory in the company history, allowing our team to reach beyond the Austin market and serve nonprofits across the U.S. so we can do more in more places.
